PROCESS

Debrief

We start off with a questionnaire, which includes inquiries involving the client's brand and vision as well as any deliverables needed to make the web designs more personal to the client. After submission, we set aside a meeting to discuss the client's needs and schedule, ensuring that we both have all the necessary information we need before commencing the next step.

Research

Taking all the information gathered from the debriefing, I research and gather information involving the market and the brand's competitors. I search for web design trends to keep the client's site modern and inviting and organize a mood board to highlight my vision for the brand.

Ideation & Revision

After research, I start putting my ideas down on digital paper. Using Figma as my go-to design software, I wireframe and create mockups. Once finalized, I update the client and proceed with the design for other pages, using the homepage as a guide.

Development

Once the client is satisfied with the finalized design, I begin the development phase. I ensure responsiveness, functionality, and performance across all devices before deployment, providing the client with a detailed feature guide.
